Margherita Pizza Recipe!!!!

Recipe Description

I absolutely love to make this homemade pizza especially with my partner, recipe together maybe person can make the dough while the other preps the toppings. It's also great for entertaining because guests can customize their final meals.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Heaping 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh bacconcini mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Extra-virgin olice oil, for drizzling

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 500 degrees farenheit. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough. Top with the fresh mozzarella and tomatoes and bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of red pepper flakes. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.
